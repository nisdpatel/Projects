My Home Country India

India is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh largest country by area, the second-most populous country with over 1.2 billion people, and the largest democracy in the world. India ia a federal constitutional republic governed under a parliamentry system consisting of 29 states and 7 union territories.


History of the Republic of India

India is famous for being one of the most ancient civilization centers of the world. Second, India is renowned worldwide for its many gods and their rich history and influence on life, even in modern times. Third, the scenic beauty of India's landscape attracts millions of tourists to the country annually. It also boasts hundreds of ancient and modern temples and shrines that intrigue visitors to the subcontinent.
